2. Why Organic Farm products are important?

Answer – Availing you good organically grown food is our motive. The organic farming system in India is not new and is being followed from ancient times. It is a method of a farming system that primarily aimed at cultivating the land and raising crops in such a way, as to keep the soil alive and in good health by use of organic wastes (crop, animal and farm wastes, aquatic wastes) and other biological materials. Nowadays, Consumers purchase organic foods for many different reasons. Many want to buy food products that are free of chemical pesticides or grown without conventional fertilizers. To fulfill this purpose of society we have established the Shetivala – Online vegetable market.
